My Super hero is Tan Macaroni Penguin

The macaroni penguin averages about 20 to 28 inches in length and about 11 pounds in weight. It has black upperparts, white underparts, and distinctive yellow and black plumes on the top of its head. This species closely resembles the royal penguin, but has a black chin rather than a white one.
